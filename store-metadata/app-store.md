## 앱 설명 (English)

### Promotional Text
> Learn English words through games! A research-based vocabulary game for kids with 360 words, 8 categories, and spaced repetition.

### Description
> **WordPop — Learn English Words by Playing!**
>
> WordPop is an English vocabulary learning game designed for elementary students based on educational research. Guess letters, discover words, and naturally build your English vocabulary through fun gameplay.
>
> **Key Features:**
> - 3 difficulty levels (4, 5, 6-letter words)
> - 8 word categories (animals, food, school, nature, body, home, action, feeling)
> - 8 types of smart hints
> - Spaced repetition review system
> - 21 achievement badges
> - Daily word challenge
> - Practice mode (5-10 min sessions)
> - Hand-drawn crayon design
>
> **Privacy First:**
> - Fully offline — no internet required
> - No data collection — everything stays on device
> - No ads, no in-app purchases
>
> 360 curated English words to build vocabulary one game at a time!
